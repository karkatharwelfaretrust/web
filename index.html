About Karkathar Welfare Trust

Karkathar Welfare Trust is a registered non-profit organization committed to creating positive social impact through professional collaboration. We don't maintain a large staff; instead, we connect those in need with accomplished professionals who can provide expert assistance.

Our approach ensures that beneficiaries receive high-quality, specialized support across education, employment, entrepreneurship, marriage, and healthcare domains.

Note: The Trust reserves the right to offer or accept assistance based on individual circumstances and available resources.

Cancellation & Refunds Policy

Last updated: January 01, 2025

Thank you for choosing Karkathar Welfare Trust. We are committed to providing quality services to our community members. Please read this Cancellation and Refunds Policy carefully.

Digital Services

Karkathar Welfare Trust provides exclusively digital services including educational assistance, employment counseling, entrepreneurship mentoring, marriage assistance, healthcare guidance, and event registrations. We do not deal in physical products.

General Refund Policy

Due to the nature of our digital services and community work, we generally do not provide refunds once services have been initiated or events have been organized. However, we consider refund requests under specific circumstances as outlined below.

Event Registrations

For event registrations (such as Mangala Sandhippu 2025):

  • Cancellation 15 days before event: 75% refund
    • Cancellation 7 days before event: 50% refund
  • Cancellation less than 7 days before event: No refund
  • Event cancellation by organizers: Full refund
  • Event postponement: Amount will be adjusted for rescheduled event

Consultation Services

For consultation and guidance services:

  • Cancellation before service initiation: 90% refund
  • Cancellation after first session: 50% refund
  • Cancellation after second session: No refund

No-Refund Scenarios

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Services already rendered or consultations completed
  • Digital materials and resources already provided
  • Event registrations where participation materials have been prepared
  • Cases of user-provided incorrect information
  • Voluntary non-participation after registration

Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days through the original payment method. Bank or payment gateway charges may be deducted from the refund amount.

How to Request Cancellation/Refund

To request a cancellation or refund:

  1. Send an email to karkatharwelfaretrust@gmail.com
  2. Include your registration details and payment reference number
  3. State the reason for cancellation/refund request
  4. We will respond within 3 business days

Special Considerations

Karkathar Welfare Trust may consider exceptional cases for full or partial refunds based on genuine emergencies or unforeseen circumstances at the discretion of the trust management.
