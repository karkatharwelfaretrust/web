About Karkathar Welfare Trust

Karkathar Welfare Trust is a registered non-profit organization committed to creating positive social impact through professional collaboration. We don't maintain a large staff; instead, we connect those in need with accomplished professionals who can provide expert assistance.

Our approach ensures that beneficiaries receive high-quality, specialized support across education, employment, entrepreneurship, marriage, and healthcare domains.

Note: The Trust reserves the right to offer or accept assistance based on individual circumstances and available resources.
